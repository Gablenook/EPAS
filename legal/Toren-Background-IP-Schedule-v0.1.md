## 1. Intended Use of This Schedule

This schedule is intended to be attached to or incorporated into the later IP Ownership and Contribution Agreement between Kevin / Toren and Fernando / SmartLockerAP / SmartLocker, LLC.

The goal is to make clear that the Toren platform, EPAS, related documentation, architecture, workflows, source code, figures, naming, product strategy, deployment model, and commercial packaging existed as Kevin / Toren Background IP and are not transferred, jointly owned, or diluted merely because they are discussed, reviewed, integrated, debugged, implemented, or commercialized with SmartLockerAP or SmartLocker, LLC.

This schedule should be reviewed and completed by counsel before signature.

Kevin Booth / Toren owns or controls the Toren Background IP described in this schedule, subject only to any expressly identified third-party components, open-source components, employer-owned materials, customer-owned materials, or excluded materials listed in Section 14.

Unless expressly agreed in a signed written agreement:

- Toren Background IP remains owned by Kevin Booth / Toren.
- Access to Toren Background IP does not create ownership rights in Fernando, SmartLockerAP, SmartLocker, LLC, Penco Products, customers, installers, vendors, channel partners, or other collaborators.
- Technical collaboration, troubleshooting, integration, code review, customer deployment, AI-assisted development, or commercial discussion does not create implied joint ownership.
